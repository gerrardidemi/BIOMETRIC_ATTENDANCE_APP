###  What a Biometric App Is Meant to Do**

A **biometric app** is designed to **identify or authenticate** individuals using their **unique biological traits**, such as fingerprints, facial features, voice, or iris patterns. The main purpose of the app is to provide **secure and convenient access** to devices, applications, or services by verifying a personâ€™s identity without needing passwords or PINs.

It works by capturing and storing biometric data, then comparing it during future logins or transactions. These apps are widely used in areas like **mobile security**, **banking**, **government ID systems**, and **healthcare** to enhance security and prevent unauthorized access.
